About Necktapers

We offer more than just a haircut !

The story of Neck Tapers' journey was not just about creating a business; it was about creating a place where people from all walks of life would feel seen, heard, and celebrated.

The vintage-inspired interior, with every piece and detail handpicked, transports you to a time when a visit to the barber was more than just a haircut. It was an experience, a ritual.
